Game Rules

  • Spin the wheel to receive a question.
  • Select letters to guess the answer.
  • You have three chances to guess incorrectly.
  • If you guess correctly, you earn points based on the wheel segment.
  • Reach 1000 points to win the game.
  • If you guess incorrectly three times, the game is over.
